# Attention Is All You Need

Vaswani et al., 2017. Introduced the [[Deep Learning/Transformers/Transformers|transformer]] architecture based purely on [[Deep Learning/Transformers/Attention|attention]], replacing recurrence and convolutions in sequence modeling. The foundation of [[Deep Learning/Transformers/GPT|GPT]], [[Deep Learning/Transformers/BERT|BERT]], and modern LLMs.
